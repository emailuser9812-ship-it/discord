Ticket Rules

Please follow these rules when creating a support ticket. Failure to follow rules may result in your ticket being closed or delayed.
  1. Be respectful to staff — abusive language will get your ticket closed.
  2. Include a clear title and a detailed description of the issue.
  3. Attach screenshots or recordings if available.
  4. Check support times before opening time-sensitive tickets.
  5. Do not open duplicate tickets — wait for a response.
  6. Follow instructions from staff and provide requested logs or files.
  7. Do not share personal or payment information in tickets.
